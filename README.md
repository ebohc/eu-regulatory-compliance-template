## Regulations covered

| Regulation | Applies to | In force |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| DORA | Financial entities and critical ICT providers | January 2025 |
| EU AI Act | Providers and deployers of AI systems in the EU | August 2026 (high-risk) |
| CSRD | Large EU companies and significant non-EU companies | 2024–2028 phased |
| NIS2 | Essential and important entities across 18 sectors | October 2024 |

---

## What's in each regulation tab

Every tab contains:

- **Article / Requirement** — the official regulatory reference
- **Obligation area** — the compliance domain being addressed
- **Requirement** — plain-English description of what is required
- **Analyst — What to implement** — what to build, configure, and document
- **Analyst — Evidence to maintain** — what documentation to keep
- **Auditor — Evidence requested** — what a regulator or assessor will ask for
- **Auditor — What a PASS looks like** — the exact standard required
- **Auditor — Common failures** — the most frequent reasons organisations fail
- **Review frequency** — how often the obligation must be reviewed
- **RAG status** — Green / Amber / Red dropdown with conditional formatting

---

## Additional tabs

**Regulation Map** — 22 obligation areas mapped across all 4 regulations. 
Shows where requirements overlap so you can satisfy multiple regulations 
with a single program.

**Glossary** — 31 EU-specific terms defined in plain English — TLPT, 
double materiality, DNSH, Scope 1/2/3, essential entity, high-risk AI, 
and more.
